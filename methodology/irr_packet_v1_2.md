# Continuity Engine: Inter-Rater Reliability (IRR) Packet v1.2

## Purpose

You are calibrating a measurement instrument. Your job is to read 60 text samples and score them across eight dimensions of the Mid-Process Identity Loop framework.

You are not a therapist. You are not diagnosing these writers. You are scoring specific, mechanical language patterns.

## Prime Directives

1. Code the text, not the subtext.
2. A high score is not a moral failing.
3. Beware the Data Baseball Bat.
4. Distinguish Boundary from Defense.
5. Do not overthink.
6. Score each sample independently.

## Scoring Scale

- 0 = Absent
- 1 = Weak / Implied
- 2 = Clear
- 3 = Strong / Dominant

## Eight Dimensions

1. Self-Reference Density (SR)
2. Identity Fixity (IF)
3. Prediction / Expectation Language (PR)
4. Threat Weighting (TH)
5. Feedback Integration (FI)
6. Narrative Flexibility (NF)
7. Fragmentation / Contradiction (FR)
8. Updated Continuity (UC)

## Loop States

- Flexible
- Integrated
- Defensive
- Rigid
- Fragmented
- Overloaded
- Stable/Neutral
- Mixed / Review Needed

## Tie-Breaker Rules

- Flexible vs. Integrated: choose Integrated only with explicit self-understanding or identity-continuity revision.
- Defensive vs. Discernment: choose Defensive for blame, dismissal, or contempt; choose Flexible when rejection is paired with evidence, partial accountability, or boundary-setting.
- Fragmented vs. Integrated: insight is not integration if the text says the pattern remains unresolved.
- Fragmented vs. Overloaded: choose Overloaded when agency, meaning, or action collapses.
- Rigid vs. Defensive: choose Rigid when fixed self-claims dominate; choose Defensive when external blame dominates.
- Stable/Neutral vs. Hidden Self-Model: third-person self-reference can still count as self-modeling.

## Confidence

- High = clear markers and one strong state
- Medium = one dominant state plus secondary signals
- Low = ambiguous, sarcastic, short, or evenly split

## Response Sheet Columns

```text
Sample ID, SR, IF, PR, TH, FI, NF, FR, UC, Primary State, Secondary State, Confidence, Notes
```

## Agreement Targets

- Primary state agreement: 75%+
- Dimension score agreement within ±1: 80%+
- Fleiss' kappa: 0.60+ acceptable, 0.75+ strong
